# Legal & Compliance Guardrails
## KYC (Know Your Customer)
All business customers must provide identification and a valid use case before accessing the Residential IP pool.
## Prohibited Traffic (The Shield)
The Gateway must drop all traffic directed to:
- Government and Military domains.
- Banking and Financial institutions.
- Known illegal content repositories.
## Transparency
- Users can opt-out at any time via the application toggle.
- Provide a monthly "Impact Report" to peers showing exactly how much bandwidth was used.
